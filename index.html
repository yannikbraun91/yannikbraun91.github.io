What is it?

Every diaper change
builds something epic.

Diaper Tower turns the most unglamorous part of parenting into a surprisingly satisfying game. Log a diaper and add it to your tower — watch it grow past a basketball hoop, a giraffe, the Eiffel Tower, and Mount Everest. One change at a time.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🧱
Visual Diaper Tower
Each logged diaper becomes a building block for your tower which gently sways as it grows. A real-world meter scale shows your exact height.
🌳
Backyard Scenery
A hand-drawn backyard with sky gradients and grass. Dark mode turns it into a moonlit night with twinkling stars.
📋
Diaper Log
Browse all changes grouped by day. Edit time, size, wet/dirty status, and notes for any entry.
📊
Statistics
14-day change chart, daily averages, and weekly trends — automatically built from your logged entries.
📦
Inventory Tracking
Scan a diaper package barcode or enter manually. The count decrements with each log and warns when stock runs low.
👶
Multi-Child Support
Track multiple children, each with their own tower, badge history, and entries. Inventory is shared across all children.
⌚
Apple Watch
Log a change directly from your wrist. The companion app syncs instantly via WatchConnectivity.
